Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Attune ("we", "our", or "us") is a health and fitness app designed for women 35 and older. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Attune:

Account data: email address and basic profile details you provide during signup.

Health and fitness data: menstrual cycle dates, workout logs, meal entries, body measurements, and any other health information you choose to enter.

Usage data: app interactions and feature usage collected through Amplitude analytics to help us improve the app.

Purchase data: subscription status managed through RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide personalized workout plans, meal plans, cycle tracking, and AI-generated insights within the Attune app.

We use anonymized analytics data to understand how the app is used and to improve features.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure hosted in the Asia Pacific region (Seoul, South Korea). We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

Attune uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Supabase for database and authentication.

RevenueCat for subscription management.

Amplitude for anonymized usage analytics.

USDA FoodData Central for nutritional information lookups.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Attune is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
